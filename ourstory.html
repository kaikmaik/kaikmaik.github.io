Our Story

It all began with Aria, my nine year old schipperke. I adopted her at age 4, Aria grew up eating premium dog as well as cooked food three times a week. When Aria was 6 years old she suddenly began having severe diarrhea: I took her to several vets and many tests were ran.

One month and numerous vet bills later, Aria was diagnosed with irritable bowel syndrome.

Make sure that your pooch's vaccinations are current.
